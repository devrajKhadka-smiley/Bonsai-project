Know more about us...



Our team is a group of five very innovative people who have come together to make this Bonsai e-commerce business a reality. All of us come from very different backgrounds and fields of study, and met each other through different friend circles. The one thing that unites us all however, is our passion for decorative nature. It was through pure coincidence that we started talking about nature, which eventually led to us forming a plan for an e-business together. Each one of us have a particular and very important contribution to this project and are very proud of it.
