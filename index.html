Decorate with us


The idea is to trim the plant's roots and leaves such that it stays dwarfed or is pruned to be that way. In particular, species of perennial trees or shrubs with woody stems that generate true branches and can be trained to remain tiny by container confinement with crown and root trimming are used to make bonsai.

Growing plants in a pot is the art and science of bonsai. A giant tree is reduced in size to a little or miniature tree to create an artistic effect. To stay alive, bonsai requires a frequently changing media in a small container. To create a unique plant, numerous species from diverse genus were traditionally crossed. It's a smaller version of a real tree, to put it simply.
